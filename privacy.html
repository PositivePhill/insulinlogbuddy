emoji_eventsOur Promise

We built Insulin Log Buddy because many of us live with diabetes too. We know your health data is deeply personal, so we protect it with the care, security, and respect it deserves—always.

inventory_2Data We Collect

  • Insulin doses, glucose levels, carb intake – only what you type in.
  • App preferences – theme, reminders, game credits.
  • Optional crash reports – anonymous technical info to fix bugs fast.

lockWhere It Lives

Your records stay on your phone in an encrypted database (Android Room). We never send them to our servers or share them with anyone.

share_offSharing & Third-Party Access

  • No Ads: 100 % ad-free. Zero trackers.
  • No Data Brokers: We never sell or trade your info.
  • Optional PDF exports: Share reports only if and when you decide.

vpn_keyPermissions

  • Notifications: Gentle reminders for logging and meds.
  • Storage (optional): Needed only to save PDF reports you create.

toggle_onYour Controls

  • Erase data anytime via Settings → Erase Local Data.
  • Uninstalling deletes all stored health data.
  • Toggle crash reporting under Settings → Privacy.

medical_informationDisclaimer

Insulin Log Buddy is a personal tracking tool and not a medical device. Always follow professional medical advice. In an emergency, dial your local emergency services.
